How to Play:

Connect Four is a strategic two player game
in which the players alternate turns dropping
a chip into a slot on the board.

The object of the game is to be the first to
get four of your chips in a row horizontally,
vertically or diagonally. At the same time you
must stop your opponent from getting four of
their own chips in a row.

How to Win:
	
A player wins by being the first to get four 
of their chips to line up.
